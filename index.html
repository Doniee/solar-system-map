The Solar System Cycle Trail

Hop on your bicycle and visit all the planets in a miniature solar system

Info

The Solar System Scale Model

  • Located in the cities of Helsinki and Espoo, Finland.
  • Scale of the Solar System model is 1:1 000 000 000 (1 mm on the ground = 1 000 km in space).
  • Built in 1992, so Pluto is still included.
  • The locations have scale models of the Sun and all of the planets. The sizes of the models and their distances are all to scale.
  • The distance between Pluto and the Sun is 6.1 km. The diameter of the model of the Sun is 140 cm. The diameter of Pluto is only 2.2 mm.
  • The cycling trail is 25 km long.
  • The suggested trail is based on the bicycle paths shown in the Helsinki Metropolitan Cycling And Outdoor Map.
